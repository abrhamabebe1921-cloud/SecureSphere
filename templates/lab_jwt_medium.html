Exploitation Guide
  1. Acquire a user token by logging in.
  2. Crack the HS256 secret using a tool like hashcat or jwt_tool.
  3. Forge a new token with "role":"admin" and sign it with the cracked secret.
  4. Update your cookie and refresh to capture the flag.
